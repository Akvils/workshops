Form from Formless:
Creating Images from Ideas 🎨

Create inspired art from your consciousness. Drawing from ancient traditions, this workshop teaches you how to channel creative energy directly from the soul to produce art that carries deep spiritual resonance.
How do symbols take shape? How do abstract ideas transform into meaningful visuals?
This workshop explores an ancient methodology described in the Upanishads, traditionally used to create sacred symbols, mantras, and imagery. You will learn how these principles can be applied to modern creative practices, from icon and logo design to spiritual and artistic expression.

What You’ll Learn:
💡 The process of translating ideas into visual forms
🕉 Ancient principles behind icon and symbol creation
🤝 A hands-on exercise to create your own meaningful art and design

Who is this for?
Designers, artists, spiritual seekers, and anyone curious about the deeper aspects of creativity.
