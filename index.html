Consciousness Technology FREE Workshops

There is a shift in our way of being when we experience the divine presence. These workshops have arisen from that space. Each one is unique, inspired, and gives you an experience of what it feels like to go beyond being a little human. They give you a taste of a way of being outside of our conditioned and programmed mind.
Please go through the descriptions and register for the events that resonate with you. Write to us if you want one in your community or have any feedback or suggestions. Even though this is a gift, we are accepting donations, for you receive ten times what you give.

Empty: Flush out psychic garbage
